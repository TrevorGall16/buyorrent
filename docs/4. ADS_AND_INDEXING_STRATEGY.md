# 4. ADS & INDEXING STRATEGY

## 1. SEO & Traffic Protocol
* **Architecture:** Static Site Generation (SSG). 46 City Pages pre-rendered.
* **URL Structure:** `domain.com/[city-slug]/buy-vs-rent`.
* **Metadata Strategy:**
    * **Title:** "Buy vs. Rent in [City] ([Year] Calculator)"
    * **Description:** Includes dynamic data: "Avg Price: $[Price]. Avg Rent: $[Rent]."
* **Structured Data:** Inject 4 Schemas per page: `SoftwareApplication`, `FinancialProduct`, `BreadcrumbList`, `WebPage`.

## 2. AdSense Implementation (CLS-Safe)
* **Golden Rule:** Ad slots have rigid `min-height` and `background: #f9f9f9`.
* **Placements:**
    1.  **Sidebar (Desktop):** Sticky, 300x600px.
    2.  **In-Content (Mobile):** 300x250px, placed between Inputs and Chart.
* **Loading:** Strategy `afterInteractive`.

## 3. The "High-Value" Content Rule
* **Problem:** Calculator pages risk "Low Value Content" rejection.
* **Solution:**
    * "How It Works" section must be visible on every city page (below the fold).
    * **Reset Protocol:** If user clicks "Reset" or changes inputs, do NOT clear the explanatory text.
